What We’ll Learn

We’re going to find out how to make pancakes and waffles! This page contains ingredient lists and directions for pancakes and waffles. Let’s start with pancakes and then we’ll go on to waffles.

Pancakes

Pancakes are amazing. Only silly people don’t like pancakes.

Pancake Ingredients

We need 5 ingredients to make fluffy pancakes. This list will tell us what we need to get.

  • Butter
  • Batter
  • Honey
  • Jam
  • Bananas (thinly-sliced)

Pancake Directions

Making pancakes isn’t hard, here’s the directions.

  1. Pour batter into a large bowl.
  2. Add in the thinly-sliced bananas.
  3. Add butter if desired.
  4. Heat a frying pan or griddle over medium to high heat.
  5. Serve hot.
  6. Repeat for each pancake.

That’s it! Now you’re ready to make your own fluffy and warm pancakes. Let’s move on to waffles now.

Waffles

Waffles are amazing. Only silly people don’t like waffles.

Waffle Ingredients

We need 5 ingredients to make crispy waffles. This list will tell us what we need to get.

Tap here for ingredients.
  • Butter
  • Batter
  • Honey
  • Jam
  • Blackberries

Waffle Directions

Making waffles isn’t hard, here’s the directions.

Tap here for directions.
  1. Pour batter into a large bowl.
  2. Add in the blackberries.
  3. Add butter if desired. (Can you have too much?)
  4. Heat a waffle iron until hot.
  5. Serve waffle hot.
  6. Repeat for each waffle.

That’s it! Now you’re ready to make your own crispy waffles.
